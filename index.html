What is DPC

Direct Primary Care is a new model of practicing medicine. Doctors charge a low monthly fee ($60-$100) directly to their patients. No insurance, no hospital admins - no headaches! This allows physicians to mantain a panel of 600 patients. Fewer patients means you can practice medicine the way you want.

How HealthSpace works

Starting a new practice can be challenging - we're here to remove those challenges. We provide you with a move-in ready space and resources to start, grow, and mantain your practice.
